WHY I LOVE VILLENEUVE'S SCI-FI FILMS

I was first introduced to Villeneuve’s films when I watched Arrival in my home for the first time. While I do believe the movie theater experience is one of a kind, I also think that Villeneuve made this film to capture an audience, np matter if it’s in the movie house or not. I was truly in awe of how perfect each scene looked visually, and I knew from there, I wanted to see more of his work.

It’s not only the visuals that make me love Villeneuve’s work, but the music as well. I am a huge fan of film scores and soundtrack. my favorite composer being Hans Zimmer. And so, every time I see “Directed by Denis Villeneuve” and “Music by Hans Zimmer” that’s when I know, I’m going to be watching a cinematic masterpiece. Arrival, Blade Runner and Dune are all rich in color and sound, and completely transport you into their worlds, and leave you wanting more of it by the end. I like the way Villeneuve’s films make me want to learn more about the each movie universe, and makes me even pick up a book for once, in search of more information about the characters and their backgrounds. So, with that said, I would like to go more in depth about my favorite sci-fi films by Villeneuve.

I will be reviewing Blade Runner 2049 (2017), Arrival (2016), and Dune: Part One (2021).
